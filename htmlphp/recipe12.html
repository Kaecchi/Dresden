Title: green coctail recipe number: 12
author: Kamil Kowalczyk
Spread the kale with boiling water and then with cold water. Place in strainer for strain. Remove hard nerves from the leaves and drop into the blender container.
Peel the cucumber, cut into smaller pieces and add to the container.
Mix ingredients in a blender with lemon juice, honey and water until a smooth consistency of the cocktail.
If the cocktail is too thick, add more water and mix again.
